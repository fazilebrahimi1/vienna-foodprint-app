## Overview
Vienna FoodPrint is a prototype app designed to help citizens reduce the material footprint of their food consumption. The app supports more sustainable food choices through meal suggestions, product scanning, food swaps, and city-level impact information.

## Problem
Food consumption is a major driver of Europe’s material footprint. Citizens often lack simple, accessible information about the environmental impact of the foods they buy and eat.

## Solution
Vienna FoodPrint helps users:
- scan food products and view their footprint score
- discover lower-impact food alternatives
- explore sustainable meal ideas
- understand the city-wide impact of dietary shifts
- learn about food sustainability and policy solutions

## Key Features
- **Food Scanner**: scan products and view material footprint scores
- **Food Swaps**: compare high-impact foods with lower-impact alternatives
- **Meal Ideas**: discover low-footprint meals
- **Vienna Impact**: simulate city-wide impact of dietary change
- **Learn & Policies**: educational section on food systems and policy tools
